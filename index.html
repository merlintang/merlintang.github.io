About me

I'm a final year PhD student at Computer Science Department of Purdue University, West Lafayette, Indiana. I am working with Professor Walid G. Aref on database system.
My research interest is database management system, similarity query processing, data indexing, big data analysis, data mining and machine learning.
Now, I am building a distributed in-memory spatial data processing system over Spark, more details can be found from our project webpage SpatialRDD.
